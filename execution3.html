Governor Sandoval's message to businesses

Governor Brian Sandoval and the Governor's Office of Economic Development (GOED) are committed to promoting a robust, diversified and prosperous economy in Nevada, stimulating business expansion and retention, encouraging entrepreneurial enterprise, attracting new business and facilitating community development. GOED supplies resources to achieve these goals through its two primary divisions, two primary divisions—the Nevada Film Office and Economic Development—and the many resource programs described in this section that can help businesses and Nevada’s economy to grow.

Contact Us

The Nevada Governor’s Office of Economic Development is committed to providing access to public records in accordance with Nevada Revised Statutes Chapter 239. Before submitting a request, please check the GOED website as certain records and data maintained by the office are available online for public viewing and/or downloading free of charge.
